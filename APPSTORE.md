## Long Description

**Workout Notes** is a simple, fast, and privacy-focused workout tracker designed for people who want to log their exercises without the complexity of predefined plans or subscriptions.

**No accounts. No subscriptions. No internet required.**

Your data stays on your device, giving you complete control over your fitness journey.

### LOG YOUR WAY
- Track any exercise: weights, reps, distance, time, or any combination
- Add notes to individual sets to remember how it felt
- Voice logging lets you record sets hands-free (say "100 by 10" to log 100kg x 10 reps)
- Configurable rest timer with auto-start between sets

### TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
- Automatic personal best detection with celebration animations
- View your estimated one-rep max (1RM) using the Epley formula
- Progress charts show your improvement over time
- Calendar view highlights your workout days at a glance

### STAY ORGANIZED
- Clean, tabular view of your sets on the home screen
- Swipe between days to review past workouts
- Agenda view shows your workout history chronologically
- Category pills help you see what muscle groups you trained

### YOUR DATA, YOUR CONTROL
- All data stored locally on your device
- Export your entire workout history to CSV
- Backup and restore your database anytime
- No account required, no data sent to servers

### DESIGNED FOR THE GYM
- Dark mode for low-light environments
- Large, easy-to-tap buttons
- Quick set entry with +/- buttons or direct input
- Haptic feedback confirms your actions

Whether you're a beginner starting your fitness journey or an experienced lifter tracking PRs, Workout Notes gets out of your way so you can focus on what matters: your workout.
