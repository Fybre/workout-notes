# Privacy Policy

## Overview

Workout Notes respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

## Data Collection

Workout Notes is an offline-first application that prioritizes your privacy:

- **No Personal Information Collected**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you
- **No Analytics**: We do not use any analytics services to track your usage
- **No Advertising**: We do not display advertisements or collect data for advertising purposes
- **No Network Requests**: The application operates entirely offline and does not make any network requests

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage Only**: All workout data is stored exclusively on your device
- **SQLite Database**: Workout logs and exercise definitions are stored in a local SQLite database
- **No Cloud Sync**: Data is not automatically synced to any cloud service
- **User-Controlled Backups**: You can manually create backups of your data to your device storage

## Data Usage

The data you enter is used solely for the purpose of:

- Tracking your workout progress
- Displaying your exercise history
- Calculating personal bests
- Providing unit conversions based on your preferences

## Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. Your workout information remains private and secure on your device.

## Data Security

- **Local Encryption**: Your data is stored locally on your device with standard mobile platform security measures
- **No External Access**: Since data never leaves your device, there's no risk of data breaches from external servers
- **Backup Security**: Manual backups are stored in your device's file system with standard file permissions

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **View Data**: You can view all your workout data within the application
- **Export Data**: You can export your workout data to CSV format
- **Delete Data**: You can delete individual workouts or clear all data
- **Backup Data**: You can create manual backups of your entire database

## Children's Privacy

Workout Notes is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Fybre/workout-notes/issues).

## Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 2025.

## Third-Party Services

Workout Notes does not use any third-party services that collect information to identify you.
